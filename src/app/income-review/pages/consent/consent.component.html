Consent Declaration

I certify that the information given in this application form and any documentation attached to it is true, correct, and complete.

I declare that my family's current net income is at least 10% lower than the net income used to calculate the Fair PharmaCare coverage I now have.

I consent to allow the Canada Revenue Agency to release information from my income tax returns and, if applicable, other required taxpayer information, to the B.C. Ministry of Health and Health Insurance BC.

The information provided will be used solely to determine, verify and administer my and/or my family's Fair PharmaCare Plan coverage under the British Columbia Pharmaceutical Services Act.

I further agree that if the Canada Revenue Agency later confirms that my net income was higher than declared in this application, I will repay the Ministry of Health any assistance I received in excess of the assistance for which I was eligible.

Consent must be provided.
