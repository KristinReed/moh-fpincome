Upload your supporting documents

  • All documents must indicate gross income and have been issued in 2020.
  • Provide documents for all income sources for you and your spouse/common-law partner.
  • Upload each document once only. If a document supports income for more than one period, submit it once for the earliest applicable period.
  • If you are unable to obtain the required supporting documents because of limited services due to the COVID-19 pandemic, you must provide a letter signed by you (and your spouse/common-law partner, if applicable) declaring the document(s) you are unable to provide, the reason they are not available, the source of the income, the gross monthly amount of this income, and how long you expect to collect this income. Upload the letter to any section below.
  • Your supporting documents and letter of declaration must account for the total income you are declaring for income review.

Document upload requirements

Scan the document, or take a photo of it. Make sure that it's:
  • The entire document, from corner to corner
  • Rotated correctly (not upside down or sideways)
  • In focus and easy to read
  • A JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP or PDF
